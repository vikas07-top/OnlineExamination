Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I log in?

To log in, use your registered email and password. If you have forgotten your password, click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page to reset it.

2. How do I start an exam?

For users: Navigate to the "Start Exam" section in your dashboard and select an available exam to begin.

3. How can I view my results?

For users: Go to the "View Results" section in your dashboard to see your exam results.

4. How do I manage users or exams?

For admins: Use the "Manage Users" or "Manage Exams" sections in your dashboard to add, edit, or delete records.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues, follow these steps:

  • Ensure your internet connection is stable.
  • Clear your browser cache and try again.
  • Ensure your browser is up-to-date.
  • If the issue persists, contact support.
